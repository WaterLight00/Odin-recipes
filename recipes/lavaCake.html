Lava Cake

A few chocolatey lava cakes

The best lave cake I've ever had. Serve these decadent molten chocolate cakes topped with poweder sugar or vanilla ice cream. This recipe makes four delicious individual desserts.

Ingredients

  • 6 1/2(1 once) squares semisweet chocolate, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup butter, cut into 8 pieces
  • 3 eggs, room temperature
  • 1/3 cup white sugar
  • 2 tablespoon all-purpose flour
  • 4 teaspoon unsweetend cocoa powder
  • 1 pinch of salt

Cooking Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F(200 degrees C). Grease and flour 6-ounce ramekins
  2. Place chocolate and butter in the top of a double boiler over simmering water. Stir frequently, scraping down the sides with a rubber spatula to avoid scorching, until chocolate is melted, about 5 minutes.
  3. Combine eggs and sugar in a large bowl; beat with and electric mixer until pale and thick, about 5 minutes. Mix melted chocolate, and cocoa powder until combined. Pour batter into prepared ramekins and place on a baking tray.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until the sides are set but the middle is still jiggly, about 10 to 13 minutes. let cool for 5 minutes before serving.
