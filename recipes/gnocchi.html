Gnocchi

Gnocci on a blue plate, ganished with parsley

Gnocchi is simple to make with just three ingredients: mashed potato, flour, and egg. This recipe is one my family has used for generations.

Gnocchi (singular gnocco) are Italian dumplings made with flour, eggs, and potatoes. Gnocchi can be served in tomato sauce, tossed with pesto, or saut√©ed with butter and herbs.

Ingredients

  • 2 potaoes, peeled
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 egg

Cooking Steps

  1. Step 1: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil; add potatoes cook until tender but still firm, about 15 minute.Drain, cool, and mash with a fork or a potato masher.
  2. Step 2: Combine 1 cup mashed potatoes, flour, and egg in a large bowl. Knead until dough forms a ball. Shape small portins of dough into long "snakes". On a floured surface, cut snakes into 1/2-in pieces
  3. Step 3: Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Drop in gnocchi and cook for 3 minutes to 5 minutes or until gnocchi have risen to the top; drain and serve.
